## Introduction

Welcome to The CatalystUI Model (“the Model”)—a universal, explanatory lens for identifying and exploring the fundamental principles of user-interface design. During a time when many frameworks prioritize solving narrowly defined, specialized problems, the CatalystUI Model takes a different approach by stepping back to examine user-interface design as a concept rather than any specific implementation.

By viewing the problem from this perspective, the Model identifies common patterns across existing solutions, reveals their limitations, and establishes a universal framework that captures the complete process of interaction between a user and an information system.
